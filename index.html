About Us

Shriji Parivar Trust, established in 2024 in the serene hills of Chail, District Solan (Himachal Pradesh), is a spiritual and charitable organization inspired by the divine grace of Goddess Radha (Shriji). The trust is committed to the service of GauMata, the underprivileged, and the environment, guided by the values of Sanatan Dharma, selfless service (seva), and community upliftment.
