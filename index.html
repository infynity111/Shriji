About Shriji Parivar Trust

Inspired by the divine grace of Goddess Radha (Shriji), we serve GauMata, the underprivileged, and the environment through selfless seva, rooted in Sanatan Dharma.

Shriji Parivaar Trust, established on 11th of September 2024 on the pious day of Radhaashtmi in amidst the serene and spiritual surroundings of Chail, nestled in the tranquil hills of District Solan, Himachal Pradesh, is a humble yet profound initiative born out of deep devotion and unwavering faith. Inspired by the boundless kindness, unconditional love, and divine compassion of Goddess Radha, the Trust stands as a beacon of selfless service and spiritual upliftment.
